Yacht Parties in Miami

There is no better way to celebrate in Miami than with a private yacht party on Biscayne Bay. Whether you are planning a birthday bash, a milestone anniversary, a bachelorette weekend, a holiday gathering, or an unforgettable New Year's Eve on the water, Miami Yacht Collective delivers a celebration experience unlike anything on land. Our fleet ranges from intimate 46-foot cruisers perfect for close gatherings of 8 to 13 guests, all the way up to a stunning 90-foot superyacht that accommodates up to 30 guests in grand style. Every yacht comes equipped with premium DJ-ready sound systems, spacious sun decks built for dancing, and comfortable lounge areas where your guests can relax between the music, the views, and the open bar.

Miami's year-round sunshine and warm waters make it the perfect city for a yacht party any time of year. Picture your group cruising past the iconic mansions of Star Island, taking in the glittering Miami skyline at sunset, and dancing under the stars as your captain navigates the calm waters of Biscayne Bay. All charters depart from Miami Beach Marina at 300 Alton Road, one of the most scenic and accessible departure points in the city. We also offer catering and bar packages so you can enjoy a fully hosted experience without lifting a finger — from gourmet bites and craft cocktails to bottle service and custom menus tailored to your event.
