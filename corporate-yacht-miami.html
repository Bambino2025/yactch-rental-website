Corporate Yacht Charters in Miami

Miami is one of the top business destinations in the United States, and there is no better way to make a lasting impression on clients, partners, and colleagues than with a private corporate yacht charter. Whether you are planning a client entertainment cruise to strengthen key relationships, a team building outing to reward your employees, a product launch that demands an unforgettable setting, or a company celebration for the holidays, Miami Yacht Collective delivers a premium experience on the water that elevates any corporate event. Our professional captains and attentive crew handle every detail so you can focus on your guests, your message, and your business objectives while cruising past the iconic Miami skyline and the turquoise waters of Biscayne Bay.

Our fleet offers yacht options up to 90 feet in length, accommodating large corporate groups of up to 30 guests in comfort and style. Every charter departs from Miami Beach Marina and can be tailored to your event, whether it is an intimate networking reception, a high-energy corporate boat party, or an elegant evening cruise for top-tier clients. We coordinate catering and bar packages designed specifically for corporate events, ensuring your team or guests enjoy a first-class dining experience on board. The privacy and exclusivity of a yacht charter gives your event something no hotel ballroom or rooftop venue can match: open water, panoramic views of Star Island and South Beach, and the freedom to create a truly unique atmosphere for your brand.

From Fortune 500 companies hosting executive retreats to startups celebrating a milestone, corporate yacht charters with Miami Yacht Collective are built around your vision. Let us help you plan a corporate event that your team and clients will talk about long after they step off the dock.
