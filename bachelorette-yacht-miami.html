Bachelorette Yacht Charters in Miami

Miami is the number one destination in the country for bachelorette parties, and there is no better way to celebrate than aboard a private luxury yacht. Miami Yacht Collective offers bachelorette yacht charters designed to give the bride-to-be and her crew an unforgettable experience on the water. Picture your bridal party cruising past the stunning mansions of Star Island, toasting with champagne against the turquoise waters of Biscayne Bay, and dancing on deck as the Miami skyline lights up at sunset. Every moment is an Instagram-worthy backdrop, from the sparkling ocean views to the golden-hour glow over Miami Beach. Our fleet accommodates groups of all sizes, from intimate bridal parties of 8 guests to large celebrations of up to 30, so whether you are planning a close-knit gathering or an all-out bash, we have the perfect yacht for your crew.

Our bachelorette charters come with everything you need to throw the ultimate bridal party on the water. Choose from champagne and rose packages to set the mood, or add full catering with gourmet bites and custom menus tailored to your group. Every yacht features DJ-ready sound systems so you can bring your own playlist or hire a DJ to keep the party going while you dance under the open sky. Departing from Miami Beach Marina, your captain will take you on a customizable itinerary past the most iconic spots in Miami, including cruises around Star Island, Fisher Island, and along the coast of South Beach, with plenty of stops for swimming, photos, and soaking in the sun. Every detail is designed to create picture-perfect memories the entire bridal party will talk about for years.

Whether you want a relaxed sunset cruise with cocktails or a high-energy yacht party with music and dancing, Miami Yacht Collective will customize every detail to match the bride's vision. Our team handles the planning so you can focus on celebrating. Browse our fleet to find the right yacht for your group, then call or WhatsApp us to lock in your date and start building your dream bachelorette charter in Miami.
